Modern Music Player
===================

Features:
- Glassmorphism card UI
- Animated album art/video
- Responsive layout
- Stylish controls and progress bar
- Playlist with clickable items
- Upload MP3/MP4
- Toast notifications
- Theme toggle (light/dark)

How to use:
- Click play/pause/next/prev to control playback
- Click a playlist item to play
- Upload your own MP3/MP4
- Toggle theme for light/dark mode
